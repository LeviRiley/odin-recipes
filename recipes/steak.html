Steak Recipe

Description

This butcher or hanger steak recipe is great pan-cooked, broiled, or grilled. It takes to marinade wonderfully and really can be substituted for any cut of steak. Just be sure to take the time to trim it well.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of butchers steak
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon clarified butter
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablesppons cold butter, cut into cubes
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. pan sear steak
  2. throw in oven
  3. take out and let rest
  4. pour butter over steak
  5. serve after sitting
